why diffusion models do not memorize
Why diffusion models generalize so well despite huge parameter counts
2 training phases
1st phase - high quality images emerge without memorization
2nd phase - a much later phase where memorization would occur if training continued.
Good generation window is long while the memorization threshold is far away. 
This explains why diffusion models produce stable non memorized outputs and outperform many other genai alternatives
